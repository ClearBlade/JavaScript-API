Example

Chaining two queries together in an or

var query1 = new ClearBlade.Query();

query1.equalTo('name', 'John');
query2.equalTo('name', 'Jim');
query1.or(query2);
//will match if an item has an attribute 'name' that is equal to 'John' or 'Jim'
